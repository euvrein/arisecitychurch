The Kid's Ministry

Contact us if you want to volunteer for the kid's ministry.

The Kid's Ministry is where we set off on a joyful adventure replete with friendship, education, and fun! Every kid is a special and priceless individual, and Kid's Ministry is committed to providing a secure and caring environment for them to develop their faith and reach their full potential.

Our ministry is made to actively involve kids of all ages in age-appropriate, interactive activities that educate them about God's love, the Bible, and the principles that form their character. Our goal is to captivate their imaginations and kindle their desire for learning more about their Creator via engaging storytelling, artistic arts & crafts, music, and activities.
