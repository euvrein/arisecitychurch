The Worship Team

Contact us if you want to join the team.

The worship team is where we assemble to lift our hearts, voices, and spirits in adoration and praise to God! We firmly think that music possesses a special ability to unite us with the holy and foster an atmosphere of awe, joy, and harmony. Our goal as a worship team is to guide the congregation through a powerful and transforming worship experience.

Our team is made up of gifted singers, musicians, and worship leaders who are enthusiastic about using their talents to serve the church body and glorify God. We try to create a worship atmosphere that is relatable to people of all ages and backgrounds by utilising a varied repertoire that includes both traditional hymns and modern worship tunes.
