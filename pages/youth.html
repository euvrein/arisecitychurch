Our Life Group, also known as Youth and Young Adults Ministry, is dedicated to fostering a vibrant community where young people can grow in their relationship with God, connect with peers who share their faith, and discover their unique purpose in serving others.

We envision a generation of young believers who are deeply rooted in their faith, empowered to make positive impacts in their communities, and equipped to navigate the challenges of today's world with grace and resilience.
